Building Pregel+

The current version of Pregel+ was tested on Centos Linux 6.4, 6.5 and Ubuntu Linux 12.04 (Pangolin). We require a 64-bit operating system.

Dependencies

We require the following dependencies.

  • g++ (>= 4.3) Required for compiling Pregel+.
  • Open MPI or MPICH3 [Strongly Recommended] Required for running distributed Pregel+ apps.
  • JDK 6 or above Required for HDFS support.
